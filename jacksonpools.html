Jackson Pools Company Website
This is a professional website I developed and helped to design for Jackson Custom Pools & Outdoor Living to showcase their portfolio of previous designs. The site was created using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap to help make it responsive. Currently, Jackson Custom Pools & Outdoor Living uses this website for advertisement and to showcase their work in client meetings to help make design decisions for future projects.

Beyond technical experience, this project helped introduce me to professional work and acclimate to essential steps of working with a client, like communicating schedules, deliverables, and issues that arose during development.
